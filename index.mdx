# C2C Merchant API

The C2C Platform lets you accept mobile wallet payments in Pakistan without a direct
JazzCash or Easypaisa gateway integration. Instead, verified members process payments
through their personal wallet accounts and confirm receipt on your behalf.

## How it works

1. **You create an order** via the API from your backend
2. **Your customer is redirected** to a hosted payment page with clear transfer instructions
3. **Your customer transfers money** to the assigned member's wallet
4. **A verified member confirms** the transfer in their app
5. **You fulfil the order** after polling for confirmation
